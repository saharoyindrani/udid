Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a UDID card in India, you must be an Indian citizen with a disability as defined by the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (RPWD) Act, 2016, with a minimum disability percentage of 40%.

🇮🇳 Basic Requirements
✓
Indian Citizenship: Must be a permanent citizen of India
✓
No Age Limit: From children to elderly can apply
✓
Free Service: No fee charged for UDID card issuance
🩺 Disability Assessment
📊
Minimum 40% Disability: As certified by authorized medical practitioners
🏥
Medical Certification: Must be assessed by government-authorized medical authorities
📜
RPWD Act 2016 Compliance: Disability must fall under recognized categories
🔍 Eligible Conditions
Eligible disabilities include conditions such as blindness, locomotor disabilities, intellectual disabilities, cerebral palsy, autism spectrum disorder, and certain mental illnesses, among others as defined by the RPWD Act 2016.

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for your UDID card online through the official government portal.

Register on Portal
Visit the official UDID portal and create your account with basic personal details and contact information.
Start New Application
Click "Apply for Disability Certificate & UDID" and begin filling out the comprehensive application form.
Complete Application Details
Fill in all required fields including personal information, identity details, and disability-related information.
Upload Required Documents
Upload clear scanned copies of Aadhaar card, address proof, photograph, and any existing medical reports.
Select Medical Authority
Choose your nearest authorized hospital or district medical board for disability assessment.
Submit & Get Application ID
Submit your application and save the Application ID for future tracking and reference.
Attend Medical Assessment
Visit the selected medical authority when called, carrying all original documents for verification.
Download Your UDID Card
After approval, track your application status and download your e-card directly from the portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about the UDID card application and usage process.

❓ Card Validity & Usage
Is UDID valid across India?

Yes, the UDID card is designed for nationwide portability and is accepted across all states and union territories.

Can I use a digital copy?

Yes, the e-card downloaded from the portal is widely accepted. However, keep a physical copy as backup when needed.

⏰ Processing & Timeline
How long does processing take?

Processing times vary by district and workload. Track your application status regularly through the online portal.

How do I track my application?

Use your Application ID to track status on the portal's tracking section.

🔄 Updates & Renewals
How do I update information?

Updates can be requested online through the portal, including address changes and re-assessments.

Is renewal required?

Cards for adults (18+) have lifetime validity. Children's cards may need renewal upon reaching adulthood.
